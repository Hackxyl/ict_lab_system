Who We Are

The Kiangini ICT Department is responsible for managing digital infrastructure, supporting computer laboratories, maintaining information systems, and enabling technology-driven learning.

We promote digital transformation, innovation, networking, ICT support, technical training, and modern technology solutions throughout the institution.
